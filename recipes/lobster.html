Steamed Lobsters

Red Steamed Lobsters, a New England classic. Not much needs to be done to have this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Water
  • Lobster

Steps

  1. Humanely kill the lobster.
  2. Fill a pot with salted water, enough to cover lobster(s), and get it to a rapid boil.
  3. Boil for 7 minutes, or until tail is completely red.
